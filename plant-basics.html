Plant Care Basics

Large Leaf Jade

  • Sunlight: Indirect
  • Water: When soil is dry an inch into it, water until it leaks out of the bottom
  • Soil: Light, well draining
  • Humidity: Moderate to high

Monstera Thai Constellation

  • Sunlight: Indirect bright light
  • Water: Soak soil and let dry fully in between
  • Soil: well draining
  • Humidity: High humidity

Stripped Brimaelaid

  • Sunlight: High indirect light
  • Water: Top water until the center cup is filled
  • Soil: Well draining
  • Humidity: High

White Wizard Philodendron

  • Sunlight: High light, partial direct to partial shade
  • Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry until it leaks through, do not let sit in standing water
  • Soil: Moisture rich soil
  • Humidity: Moderate to high

Strawberry Shake Philodendron

  • Sunlight: High direct, watch for leaf burning
  • Water: Soak well, allow to dry between watering
  • Soil: Moisture rich
  • Humidity: Moderate

Arrow Head Sygonium

  • Sunlight: High light, indirect
  • Water: Soak and allow to dry between watering
  • Soil: High PH, moisture rich
  • Humidity: Medium

Spiral Cactus

  • Sunlight: High direct or, high indirect
  • Water: Sparingly, about: 1-2 times a month or less in colder seasons
  • Soil: Well draining, dry
  • Humidity: High

Rhipsalis Burchelli

  • Sunlight: High indirect
  • Water: Soak soil, allow to dry completely between watering
  • Soil: Well draining
  • Humidity: Medium

Kalanchoe

  • Sunlight: High, partial direct, East facing sunlight
  • Water: Once a week, light watering
  • Soil: Well draining, do not let sit in standing water
  • Humidity: Medium

Drimiopsis Sunny Skies

  • Sunlight: High light, some direct
  • Water: Sparingly, about: once a week, let dry fully between watering
  • Soil: Well draining, dry
  • Humidity: High
